Evaporative Cooled Condenser Retrofit

Energy-Efficient Retrofit for Commercial Refrigeration

Project Overview

This project details the retrofit of a commercial refrigeration condenser to an evaporative cooled system, enhancing efficiency and reducing electricity consumption.

Key Features

  • Designed and implemented evaporative cooling solution for an existing air-cooled condenser.
  • Tested before and after retrofit for performance and energy savings.
  • Analyzed ROI and payback period for industrial application.
