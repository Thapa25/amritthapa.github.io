Temperature and Humidity Control in an
Evaporative Cooling AC System

Project Introduction

This project developed a combined evaporative cooling and desiccant wheel (EC/DW) system for residential temperature and humidity control, aiming for substantial energy and emissions savings compared to conventional AC systems. Objectives included:

  • Modeling: Thermal & humidity dynamics via differential equations and Simulink.
  • Control: PID & ANN strategies for setpoint tracking and disturbance rejection.
  • Evaluation: Open/closed-loop simulations and energy/emissions analysis.

Key Results

  • <3% steady-state error on temperature & humidity setpoints (closed-loop).
  • Up to 80% energy savings vs. vapor-compression AC.
  • Proved ANN viability for residential HVAC control.
