Evaporative Cooler AC with ANN Control

Low-Energy Cooling Solution for Arid Regions

Project Overview

This project delivers an energy-saving air conditioning solution by integrating evaporative cooling with Artificial Neural Network (ANN) control for optimized operation in arid climates. The design includes sensor-based feedback, automatic water control, and a focus on minimal power use and water waste.
