Closed Circuit Subsonic Wind Tunnel

Design & Analysis of a Low-Speed Wind Tunnel with Axial Fan

Project Overview

Complete design & analysis of a closed-circuit subsonic wind tunnel, including an axial fan. Methodology from literature review and analytical sizing, validated via full-channel CFD simulations across design & off-design conditions.

Abstract

Target was a 2.6 m×1.84 m test section at 100 m/s. Achieved <1% flow uniformity mid-plane (<3% overall), <0.17% turbulence intensity. Axial fan (isolated-airfoil + free-vortex) achieved 89% analytical and 80.85% CFD efficiency at ΔP = 853 Pa.
